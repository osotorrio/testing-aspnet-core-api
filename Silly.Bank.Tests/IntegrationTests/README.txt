Here you will code the Integration Tests. Understanding Integration Test as a test that cross the boundaries of your application. 

That is, a test that check your application is integrating with other components such databases, queues, other APIs and services.

Databases are easy to test because they are "close" to your application. But testing integration with other services is not easy. 

Take a look to Consumer Driven Testing. Which is heavily used in Microservices architecture.
